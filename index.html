The App

Don't look for the information,
it will reach you.

Our project was to design a dynamic
logo for an Asian company which
operates in an airport.

The project involves researching
prospective logo subjects,
visual inspiration and interpretation.

Shankar student software engineers
were part of this project as well.

We discovered that these mysterious
sea animals are among the most
intelligent and behaviorally flexible
of all invertebrates.
Regarding its complex anatomy;
The octopus has a central brain and
eight flexible tentacles each of which
has its own independent nerve
system, allowing autonomous
operation; and Because it does not
have a skeleton, the animal is very
flexible, especially in reaching anywhere.
Light signals are used to
communicate between individuals.

There are parallels between an airport passenger information system and certain characteristics of the octopus:
• Airport arrival and departure information can be collected from distributed subsystems assembled in a centralized computer and made available to passengers through independent information devices, for example an iPhone.
• The octopus can get something to eat by making a decision in its central brain, commanding one of it's independent tentacles to grab the food.

A further parallel is in the communications technology area:
• The airport passenger information system would use fiber optic cables with information represented by light signals.
• The octopus uses light signals to communicate with other octopus.

These parallels are the basis for the concept for the i reach company, an information company providing tailored information for individual passengers using an iPhone application.

i reach provides a new level of personalized airport service for passengers, who, using the i reach app can instantly find information including:

• For departing passengers: directions to security checks and the check-in counter, location of rest rooms, directions to the duty-free shopping and to food and beverage service, location and status of departure gate, and time of departure.

• For arriving passengers: location of immigration control and baggage claim, directions to customs, and ground transportation options.

The object was to design a logo that represents an innovator information company that serves the user quickly with the capability to gather from all relevant sectors for the passenger.
I took insperation from science, architecture and math in nature.
